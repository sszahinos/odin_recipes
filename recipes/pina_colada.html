Bronco Burguer

Description

A simple run-of-the-mill piña colada drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • ½ cup rum
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar
  • 8 cubes ice

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine coconut milk, pineapple juice, rum, sugar and ice. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses, and serve immediately.
